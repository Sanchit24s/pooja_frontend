About Us

Daivat Darshan Services is the number one preferred digital online booking platform for all Hindu devotional needs and spiritual services. Established to digitally connect current and future generations to Hinduism via our web portal, Android, and iOS apps, we aim to bridge the gap created by globalization and technology.

In an age where westernization threatens to overshadow our cultural roots, age-old traditions, values, and etiquettes risk extinction. Daivat Darshan's mission is to preserve and propagate the origins, history, and culture of Hinduism, ensuring they are carried forward to future generations, fostering a deeper connection to spirituality.

We connect you with experienced Pandit Jis for a variety of rituals, including Puja Path, Havan, Japs, and Dosh Nivarun. Additionally, we facilitate Janam Kundli, horoscope readings, and healing processes. Daivat Darshan is committed to guiding you on your spiritual path with dedication and reverence.
